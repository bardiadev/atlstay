## Marietta's STR Market: Historic Square, Battle Sites, and Braves Baseball

Marietta is the kind of city that rewards short-term rental owners who understand its distinct personality. This isn't a bedroom community. It's a city with its own history, its own gathering place, and its own gravitational pull — and those qualities translate directly into a demand profile that's broader and more durable than many metro-Atlanta markets.

### The Square as Anchor

Marietta Square is the civic heart of the city and one of the genuine public squares that survived Atlanta's suburban expansion era intact. The restaurants, boutiques, and green space draw local and regional visitors throughout the year. The Square hosts a regular schedule of events — from the Marietta Market to seasonal festivals to Art in the Park gatherings — that generate recurring weekend demand spikes. Guests who book near the Square often stay two to three nights to make the most of the walkable dining scene.

The Marietta Museum of History and the Marietta Gone With the Wind Museum add a heritage tourism dimension that keeps culturally motivated visitors in the booking pool year-round.

### Kennesaw Mountain: The Outdoor Heritage Visitor

Kennesaw Mountain National Battlefield Park draws over a million visitors annually, making it one of the most visited National Park units in Georgia. The combination of Civil War history and accessible hiking trails appeals to a guest profile that spans families, history enthusiasts, and casual outdoors visitors. These guests typically want to stay close to the park rather than commuting from a downtown hotel — and Marietta properties fill that need naturally.

### Truist Park and the Braves Effect

The relocation of the Atlanta Braves to Truist Park in 2017 transformed Marietta's northern edge. With 81 regular-season home games and the surrounding Battery Atlanta development hosting concerts and events separately from the baseball calendar, Truist Park is one of the most reliable demand drivers in Cobb County. Smart pricing around the Braves home schedule — and around the Battery's independent concert lineup — captures premium rates that flat-pricing approaches leave on the table.

### Business and Defense Sector Demand

Dobbins Air Reserve Base and Lockheed Martin's Marietta facility generate a steady stream of contractors, government consultants, and business travelers who prefer furnished rentals over extended hotel stays. This adds a weekday demand layer that balances the weekend-heavy leisure calendar.
