## Braselton's Short-Term Rental Market: Wine Country, Motorsports, and Northeast Metro Premium Demand

Braselton operates in a different segment than most metro Atlanta suburban markets. It doesn't rely on proximity to the city center for its demand — it generates its own through two internationally recognized destination anchors that produce guests who travel specifically to Braselton, not just through it.

### Château Élan: Georgia's Wine Country Destination

Château Élan Winery & Resort is the most significant resort property in northeast Georgia, encompassing a producing winery, multiple golf courses, a European-style spa, five restaurants, and conference facilities. Wine tourism — travelers who plan a trip specifically around vineyard visits, wine dinners, and wine-country leisure — is one of the fastest-growing segments in domestic travel. Château Élan anchors that experience for the entire Atlanta metro area.

When the resort's own rooms are occupied, when guests prefer a house setting for family groups, or when visitors extend a Château Élan trip into a multi-night Braselton stay, nearby STR properties are the natural beneficiary.

### Road Atlanta: Premier Motorsports Events

Michelin Raceway Road Atlanta is a world-class purpose-built road course with an international reputation in sports car and motorcycle racing. Petit Le Mans, run each October as part of the IMSA WeatherTech Championship, is one of the premier endurance events in North America. Race weekends produce accommodation demand that significantly exceeds what Braselton's hotel base can provide. STR properties in the area are essential inventory for these weekends.
