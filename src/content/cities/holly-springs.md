## Holly Springs' STR Market: Sports Tourism and Trail Access

Holly Springs may be one of Cherokee County's smaller cities, but its short-term rental market benefits from a demand driver that few markets can match in terms of volume and predictability: LakePoint Sports Complex.

### LakePoint: A Tournament Machine

LakePoint Sports Complex in nearby Emerson is one of the largest multi-sport youth tournament campuses in the Southeast. The facility hosts hundreds of tournaments annually across baseball, softball, soccer, lacrosse, and other youth sports — drawing families from Georgia, Tennessee, the Carolinas, and beyond for events that typically span three to four days.

These tournament families represent an ideal STR booking profile: they need multiple bedrooms for extended groups, they're not particularly interested in hotel-style amenities, and they book months in advance around a published tournament calendar. Properties in Holly Springs are among the closest comfortable options to the complex, and large-format homes — those with four or more bedrooms — can command particularly strong rates on tournament weekends.

### Trail Access and Outdoor Recreation

Holly Springs sits within easy reach of Blankets Creek, one of the most popular mountain bike trail systems in the Southeast. The trail network draws serious cyclists and recreational riders from across metro Atlanta and beyond for day trips and multi-day visits. Visitors who want to ride multiple days prefer a home base over a hotel, making Holly Springs a natural STR catchment area for the outdoor recreation segment.

### Position Between Two Established Markets

One of Holly Springs' less obvious advantages is its placement between Woodstock to the south and Canton to the north. When either of those markets fills up on a busy weekend — festival, major sporting event, holiday weekend — Holly Springs properties capture the overflow booking demand from guests who want to be in the area but couldn't find availability in their first-choice market.

The Outlet Shoppes at Atlanta, just minutes away, adds a shopping-oriented demand layer that keeps weekend occupancy consistent outside tournament season.
