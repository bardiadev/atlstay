## Fayetteville's Short-Term Rental Market: Production Hub, Healthcare Growth, and New Destination Identity

Fayetteville is undergoing the kind of transformation that STR market analysts spend years waiting to identify in advance. It's still in process, which means property owners who position correctly now will have the supply-demand advantage that typically only appears in retrospect.

### Trilith Studios: North America's Largest Purpose-Built Production Campus

Trilith Studios in Fayetteville operates at a scale that is difficult to fully appreciate without visiting. With over 1.5 million square feet of world-class production facilities and 34 premium sound stages, the campus accommodates multiple large productions simultaneously. Approximately 4,000 people work on the production campus on active production days.

That workforce needs housing. Not hotel rooms — production crews working extended engagements need furnished residences with real kitchens, functional workspaces, and enough room to decompress after 12-hour shoot days. A Fayetteville STR positioned for this segment can fill months of its calendar with a small number of high-quality, low-turnover bookings.

### The Emerging Destination Layer

Trilith Studios sparked the creation of the Trilith Town Center directly across the street — a mixed-use district with chef-driven restaurants, retail, and entertainment anchored by Trilith LIVE, a full-scale entertainment complex. The U.S. Soccer National Training Center will add tournament travel to the mix. Piedmont Fayette Hospital's planned $275 million expansion signals permanent growth in healthcare infrastructure.

These layers compound. Fayetteville is not simply a production market; it's becoming a multi-vector destination with demand that exists even between major production cycles.
