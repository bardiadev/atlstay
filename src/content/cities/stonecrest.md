## Stonecrest's Short-Term Rental Market: DeKalb's Newest City and an Early-Mover Opportunity

Stonecrest became Georgia's newest city in 2017, carved out of unincorporated eastern DeKalb County by a community ready to manage its own identity and development. That youth is both the market's greatest challenge and its clearest opportunity: limited STR competition in a corridor that is actively growing.

### Mall at Stonecrest: Retail and Event Demand

The Mall at Stonecrest is one of the largest retail anchors in eastern DeKalb County, drawing shoppers, families, and event attendees from a wide geographic radius. Retail-driven travel is an underappreciated demand source for suburban STR markets — visitors traveling for shopping, retail therapy, and major mall events (holiday seasons, special exhibitions, pop-up events) represent genuine overnight demand that doesn't require an airport or a conference center. Properties within easy reach of the mall benefit from this activity without heavy competition for bookings.

### I-20 Corporate Corridor

Eastern DeKalb and western Rockdale counties host significant distribution and logistics infrastructure. Workers on project assignments along this stretch of I-20 need practical, comfortable housing that a business hotel doesn't always provide at a reasonable price. A Stonecrest STR with solid WiFi, a home office setup, and a well-equipped kitchen can serve this segment steadily across the calendar, particularly during weekdays when leisure bookings are thinner.

### An Emerging City with Room to Define Itself

Stonecrest is still writing its identity. The city has ambitious development plans, and the commercial activity around the mall corridor is evolving. For STR hosts willing to invest in a market before it reaches saturation, Stonecrest offers the kind of first-mover opportunity that doesn't exist in more established Atlanta suburbs.

STR regulations in Stonecrest may fall under city and/or DeKalb County rules, and the framework may still be developing. See our [Atlanta-area STR guide](/resources/atlanta-short-term-rental-regulations/) for broader regulatory context.
