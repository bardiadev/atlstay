## Peachtree City's Short-Term Rental Market: One of Georgia's Most Distinctive Demand Profiles

Most short-term rental markets can be described in familiar terms: corporate demand, weekend leisure, event-driven spikes. Peachtree City is different. The combination of Trilith Studios, a one-of-a-kind golf cart community, and proximity to Hartsfield-Jackson creates a demand profile that you simply can't find anywhere else in Georgia.

### Trilith Studios and the Production Industry Stay

Trilith Studios has grown into one of the most significant purpose-built film and television production campuses in the world. Located directly in Peachtree City, Trilith houses massive production facilities that attract major studio projects year-round. With large productions come large crews: directors, producers, writers, cinematographers, set designers, wardrobe staff, and hundreds of support workers who need furnished, comfortable housing for the duration of their engagement.

Production stays are among the most desirable in the STR world. They book for weeks or months, not nights. They pay competitive rates. They require minimal day-to-day contact once settled. And they don't generate the turnover wear of a high-rotation leisure calendar. A property positioned for this segment — with quality furnishings, fast internet, and a workspace — can achieve extended-stay income that dramatically outperforms traditional Airbnb hosting.

### The Golf Cart Experience: A Genuine Differentiator

Peachtree City's 100-plus miles of multi-use paths — originally designed for golf carts — are a genuine curiosity for visitors and a beloved community feature for residents. The paths connect neighborhoods to shops, restaurants, parks, and the lakefront, creating a traffic-free way to experience the city that draws visitors specifically wanting this experience.

For STR owners, a property with golf cart access — especially with a cart available for guest use — is a clear differentiator in listing presentation. Guests book Peachtree City partly for the golf cart experience, and a listing that delivers on that expectation captures better rates and reviews.

### Airport Proximity and the Business Transit Market

Peachtree City sits roughly 20-30 minutes from Hartsfield-Jackson, the world's busiest airport. Early-morning travelers, airline crews seeking layover accommodations, and business visitors who need a Fayette County base with easy airport access all represent a demand segment that Peachtree City captures naturally. This adds a practical, utilitarian demand layer to the leisure and production stays.
