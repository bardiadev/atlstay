## Augusta, GA: The Masters and a Year-Round Rental Market Worth Managing

Augusta occupies a singular position in the Georgia short-term rental landscape. No other Georgia city has a demand event comparable to the Masters Tournament. Every April, Augusta National Golf Club hosts the most prestigious golf tournament in the world, and the city's accommodation infrastructure — hotels, bed and breakfasts, and short-term rentals — runs at absolute capacity for the week. For property owners who manage the event correctly, Masters Week can represent a defining portion of annual rental revenue compressed into seven days.

### Masters Week: What It Actually Means for STR Owners

The Masters Tournament typically runs the first full week of April. Augusta National does not permit overnight guests on the property, and the event draws a global audience of golf enthusiasts, corporate guests, and media professionals who need accommodation across the entire Augusta region. Demand extends well beyond Augusta proper — properties in North Augusta, South Carolina; Evans; Martinez; and throughout the CSRA command elevated rates during Masters Week.

The revenue opportunity is real, but so is the complexity of capturing it. Minimum-night requirements, cancellation policies, pricing by day of the week (practice rounds versus tournament rounds command different rates), and the very specific preferences of Masters guests (proximate to the course, enough bedrooms for a group, functioning kitchen for pre-round breakfasts) all require deliberate management rather than listing-and-forgetting. ATLStay builds each Augusta client's Masters Week strategy individually, calibrated to their specific property's location, size, and amenities.

### The Medical District: Augusta's Year-Round Foundation

Augusta University and AU Health form one of the largest medical complexes in the Southeast, and medical-related travel is a structural feature of the Augusta rental market that operates year-round. Patients receiving multi-week cancer treatment, cardiac care, or other specialty services frequently need accommodations for themselves and accompanying family members for weeks at a time. Extended-stay medical travel tends to produce longer bookings, more careful guests, and steady year-round occupancy that stabilizes the income profile of an Augusta property outside of the April peak.

Fort Eisenhower, the large military installation formerly known as Fort Gordon, adds another dimension: military PCS moves, family relocations, and official travel generate consistent demand from a guest profile that values reliability and cleanliness above all.

### The Riverfront and Cultural Calendar

Augusta's downtown core has invested in its Riverwalk and cultural infrastructure over the past two decades. The Augusta Canal National Heritage Area — a 19th-century industrial canal converted into a recreation and heritage corridor — attracts visitors interested in history, cycling, and kayaking. The James Brown Arena anchors an events calendar that includes concerts, sporting events, and the Augusta Futurity, the nationally known cutting horse competition held each January that fills the city's accommodation inventory during the winter.
