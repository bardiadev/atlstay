## Covington's Short-Term Rental Market: Film Tourism and the Hollywood of the South

Covington's STR market is anchored by something almost no other metro Atlanta city has: a sustained international fan tourism community tied to a long-running television series, operating alongside an active film production industry that generates ongoing crew accommodation demand.

### The Vampire Diaries Effect

The Vampire Diaries filmed its fictional Mystic Falls primarily in Covington, using the historic courthouse square and surrounding streets in hundreds of episodes over eight seasons. The show's global fanbase did something unusual: it converted viewers into pilgrimage visitors who travel specifically to Covington to stand in the filming locations they watched for years on screen.

This isn't typical pop culture tourism that peaks briefly after release. The Vampire Diaries fan community maintains organized tour infrastructure, active social media groups, and a steady visitor flow that has continued for years after the show ended. Fans travel from across the US and internationally. For a STR in Covington, these visitors represent a reliable, year-round booking segment with strong travel intent.

### Active Film Production

Georgia's screen production industry keeps Covington's historic square in active use. The same architectural character that makes the square work for fan tourists makes it an attractive filming location for new productions. Crew members on extended shoots — directors of photography, production designers, costume supervisors — prefer STR homes over hotel stays for multi-week assignments. They're reliable, professional guests with accommodation budgets that reflect industry rates.

### Historic Square and Heritage Draw

Covington's courthouse square is among the most intact antebellum courthouse complexes in Georgia. The historic district surrounding it preserves the context that makes the square meaningful — not just one building, but a surrounding commercial streetscape that reinforces the historic period.
