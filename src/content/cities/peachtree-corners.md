## Peachtree Corners' Short-Term Rental Market: Tech Hub, Corporate Demand, and Premium Positioning

Peachtree Corners occupies a unique position in the Gwinnett STR landscape. It is not primarily a leisure market — it is Georgia's designated smart city, a recognized technology innovation hub, and one of the most concentrated corporate employment corridors in the northeast Atlanta metro. For STR owners, that means a guest profile with above-average incomes, above-average accommodation expectations, and a consistent demand calendar tied to the technology sector's activity.

### Curiosity Lab: Putting Peachtree Corners on the National Map

Curiosity Lab at 147 Technology Parkway is a real-world testing environment for connected vehicles, 5G infrastructure, smart city technology, and autonomous mobility. Operated in partnership with Georgia Tech, it functions as a living laboratory where technology companies from across the country and internationally deploy teams to test and demonstrate their innovations.

The implications for STR demand are direct: Curiosity Lab attracts engineers, researchers, program managers, and corporate delegations who need accommodation for days to several weeks at a time. These guests represent the segment most likely to prefer a well-equipped private home over a business hotel, and they tend to book at higher rates and leave detailed, positive reviews for properties that meet their expectations.

### Technology Parkway and the Corporate Employment Base

The Technology Parkway corridor hosts major employers in technology, financial services, and professional consulting. The combined corporate population of Peachtree Corners generates continuous weekday STR demand that goes well beyond what Curiosity Lab alone produces. Corporate guests in this corridor want reliable infrastructure above everything: fast internet, a workspace, reliable parking, and a well-maintained property.

### The Forum and Premium Lifestyle Access

The Forum on Peachtree Parkway is Peachtree Corners' premium lifestyle shopping and dining center. Its presence within the city gives STR guests convenient access to high-quality dining and retail — a meaningful quality-of-stay factor for corporate guests on extended assignments who want to avoid driving to Atlanta for a good dinner.
