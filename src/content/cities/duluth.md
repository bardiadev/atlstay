## Duluth's Short-Term Rental Market: Cultural Diversity, Entertainment Venues, and Gwinnett's Corporate Growth

Duluth occupies a unique position in Gwinnett County — and in metro Atlanta's short-term rental landscape. Its combination of major entertainment infrastructure, a nationally known Korean-American cultural district, and growing corporate employment creates a demand profile unlike any other city in the metro.

### Gas South District and the Event-Driven Demand Floor

Gas South District (previously known as Infinite Energy Center) is one of the largest combined arena and convention facilities in metro Atlanta. The arena seats around 13,000 for concerts and major events, while the convention center portion hosts trade shows, corporate conferences, and large-scale gatherings year-round. The combination means Gas South generates demand outside the typical concert season: a trade show on a Tuesday in March is just as useful to a Duluth STR owner as a sold-out Saturday concert in July.

For Keystone Stays clients in Duluth, the Gas South calendar is a primary pricing input. Event nights — large concerts, major conventions, regional sports tournaments — drive demand spikes that flat-pricing approaches consistently underutilize.

### Duluth's Korean-American Community and Cultural Tourism

Duluth is home to one of the largest Korean-American communities in the Southeast. The concentration of Korean restaurants, grocery stores, spas, and retail along and around Duluth Highway creates a genuine cultural destination that draws visitors from Atlanta, from other parts of the country, and internationally. Culinary tourists specifically plan trips to Gwinnett County for access to authentic Korean cuisine — and Duluth is their natural base.

This is a demand segment with staying power that doesn't depend on any single event or institution. Cultural tourism to Duluth's Korean district is consistent and growing, and properties that can be positioned as convenient bases for this experience have a built-in differentiator.

### The GA-141 Technology and Corporate Corridor

The Peachtree Industrial Boulevard and GA-141 corridor through Duluth hosts growing technology and business services employment. This creates weekday demand from business travelers who prefer furnished private rentals over Gwinnett's extended-stay hotel stock.
