## Conyers' Short-Term Rental Market: Olympic Legacy, Monastic Tourism, and Historic Character

Conyers sits 24 miles east of Atlanta along I-20, and its STR market is shaped by two demand anchors that are genuinely without parallel in the metro area. No other suburb can offer guests both an Olympic-legacy equestrian facility and a centuries-old Cistercian monastery within 10 minutes of each other — and for STR hosts, this combination creates a market that operates on its own rhythm.

### Georgia International Horse Park: Event-Driven Demand

The Horse Park's event calendar is one of the most predictable demand drivers a suburban STR host can ask for. Major equestrian competitions — recognized shows, eventing competitions, dressage championships — bring participants and spectators who need accommodation for the duration of multi-day events. These guests often travel with vehicles, trailers, and equipment; they need practical, comfortable housing rather than boutique experience. They are reliable, respectful guests who return annually as the competition calendar repeats.

For hosts, the Horse Park calendar is a planning tool. Listing dates around confirmed events at premium rates — set well in advance when demand is established — is a straightforward revenue optimization that most Conyers properties are underusing.

### The Monastery of the Holy Spirit

Founded by Trappist monks in 1944 on 2,000 acres outside Conyers, the Monastery of the Holy Spirit operates one of Georgia's most visited religious heritage sites. The grounds are open to visitors, the gift shop and bonsai garden draw thousands annually, and the monastic community offers retreat programs that bring guests from across the country. Religious travelers, pilgrimage groups, and individuals seeking contemplative retreat are a niche but loyal guest segment that values quiet, private accommodation. A peaceful, well-positioned Conyers STR can serve this market with minimal friction.

### Olde Town and the I-20 Corridor

Olde Town Conyers' independent restaurant scene and event calendar provide a leisure dimension that weekend visitors appreciate. Combined with consistent I-20 corporate demand from the eastern metro corridor, Conyers has a multi-source demand base that keeps occupancy solid across the calendar.
