## Vinings' STR Market: Premium Position, Battery Proximity, Buckhead Access

The Battery's mix of nationally recognized restaurants, live music venues, and event spaces generates demand on dates with no baseball at all — a characteristic that distinguishes Truist Park's economic impact from single-purpose sports venues.

### Buckhead Access and Corporate Demand

Vinings' location on the Cobb County–Atlanta border gives it one of the most strategic positions in the metro area for corporate STR demand.
